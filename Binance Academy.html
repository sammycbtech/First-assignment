Binance Academy is a global, open-access learning platform created by Binance, the world’s leading blockchain ecosystem. Launched in 2018, its mission is to provide free, high-quality education on blockchain technology, cryptocurrencies, and financial literacy. The platform offers:
  • Comprehensive articles and tutorials covering topics like Bitcoin, Ethereum, NFTs, DeFi, trading, and Web3.
  • Interactive courses and quizzes that help learners test their knowledge and earn certificates.
  • Multilingual support, allowing users around the world to learn in their preferred language.
  • Developer-focused content, including guides on smart contracts, blockchain architecture, and security practices.
Binance Academy empowers beginners and professionals alike to explore the evolving world of digital finance and decentralized technology — completely free and ad-free.
