Brief Overview of Binance

Binance is the world's largest cryptocurrency exchange, providing a platform for users to trade over 350 cryptocurrencies. In addition to trading, it offers services like staking, futures trading, and a marketplace for NFTs, all while using its native token, Binance Coin (BNB), which can be used to pay for services on the platform and grants users discounts on trading
